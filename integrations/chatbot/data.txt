Overview:
TravelMates Hotel is a full-service, family-friendly hotel focused on comfort, convenience, and local experiences. We offer modern rooms, personalized service, and helpful travel information to ensure guests enjoy a relaxed and memorable stay.

Tone & Behavior:

Bookings & Reservations:
- Reservations can be made via the hotel's website, by phone at +1-555-0199, or in person at reception.
- Guests should provide full name, contact number, check-in and check-out dates, room type, and payment details when booking.
- Early check-in and late check-out are subject to availability and may incur an additional fee; contact reception to request.

Room Types & Features:
- Standard Room: Queen bed or two twin beds, free Wi‑Fi, 43" TV, work desk, and ensuite bathroom.
- Deluxe Room: Larger space, king bed, city or garden view, minibar, and complimentary bottled water.
- Suite: Separate living area, king bed, Nespresso machine, enhanced amenities, and priority housekeeping.
- Accessible Rooms: Available upon request with roll-in showers and grab bars.

Check-In / Check-Out:
- Standard check-in time: 3:00 PM. Early check-in may be available upon request.
- Standard check-out time: 11:00 AM. Late check-out may be arranged for a fee if rooms are available.
- Valid photo ID and a credit card or deposit are required at check-in.

Payment & Cancellation:
- We accept major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), debit cards, and cash.
- Cancellation policies vary by rate; standard refundable rates allow cancellation up to 24 hours before arrival. Non-refundable rates are charged at booking.
- For group bookings or extended stays, contact the reservations team for special terms.

Amenities & Services:
- Complimentary high-speed Wi‑Fi throughout the property.
- 24-hour front desk and concierge service.
- On-site fitness center and business center.
- Daily housekeeping; turndown service available on request.
- Laundry and dry-cleaning services (same-day on request).
- Luggage storage available before check-in or after check-out.

Dining & Food Service:
- On-site restaurant 'The Compass' serves breakfast, lunch, and dinner with local and international dishes.
- Breakfast hours: 6:30 AM – 10:30 AM; buffet and à la carte options available.
- Room service is available from 7:00 AM – 10:00 PM (menu and surcharges apply).
- A small grab-and-go pantry is open 24/7 for snacks and beverages.

Transportation & Parking:
- Valet and self-parking available; fees may apply—check with reception for current rates.
- Airport shuttle service can be arranged in advance (additional charge).
- Taxi and rideshare partners are available through the front desk; estimated travel times depend on traffic.

Local Attractions & Travel Info:
- The hotel is centrally located near the Old Town district, the Riverfront Promenade, and the Grand Museum.
- Popular nearby activities: guided city tours, bike rentals, and river cruises. Check concierge for schedules and tickets.
- Public transit: the central tram stop is a 5-minute walk; single rides and day passes are accepted on trams and buses.

Accessibility & Safety:
- Elevator access to all guest floors; some historic areas may have limited accessibility—ask the front desk for alternatives.
- Emergency exits are clearly marked; in case of emergency, follow staff instructions and use the nearest exit.
- For urgent assistance, dial '0' from your room phone or call the front desk directly.

Special Requests & Guest Services:
- Dietary restrictions: the kitchen is happy to accommodate allergies and special diets with advance notice.
- Celebrations and special requests (e.g., room decorations, cake delivery) can be arranged through the concierge.
- Business services: printing, scanning, and meeting room bookings available—contact the business center.
